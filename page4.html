            Lower Body          


Upper Body

Serratus Anterior 

O: ribs 1-9


I: medial border of scapula


N: long thoracic nerve


A: protraction

Rhomboids

O: spinous processes of C7-T5


I: medial border of scapula


N: dorsoscapulae


A: scapular retraction

Upper Trapezius

O: occipital protuberance, ligaments of the neck, base of skull

I: lateral 1/3 of clavicle, acromion, spine of scapula

N: CN 11 (accessory)

A: elevate the scapula

Lower Trapezius 

O: spinous processes of T6-T12


I: triangular space of spine of scapula


N: CN 11 (accessory)


A: depress the scapula

Middle Trapezius

O: spinous processes of C7-T5

I: triangular space of spine of scapula

N: CN 11 (accessory)

A: scapular retraction

Levator Scapulae

O: transverse processes C1-4

I: superior angle of scapula

N: dorsal scapulae

A: elevate scapula

Biceps Brachii

O: Long head: supraglenoid tubercle
 Short head: coracoid process

I: radial tuberosity

N: musculocutaneous

A: flexion and supination

Brachialis 

O: distal 1/2 of humerus

I: coranoid process

N: musculocutaneous

A: flexion

Supraspinatus 

O: supraspinatus fossa

I: greater tubercle

N: suprascapular

A: abduction to 30 degrees

Infraspinatus 

O: infraspinatus fossa

I: greater tubercle

N: suprascapular

A: external rotation

Ancoeus

O: lateral epicondyle

I: proximal ulna

N: radial

A: weak extension

Triceps Brachii

O: lateral head: upper 1/2 of humerus 
 long head: infraglenoid tubercle
 medial head: distal 1/2 of humerus

I: olecranon process

N: radial

A: elbow extension

Latissimus Dorsi 

O: posterior ilium, sacrum, spinous processes of T5-T6

I: floor of the intertubercle groove

N: thoracodorsal

A: adduction, internal rotation, extension

Teres Minor

O: lateral border of scapula 


I: greater tubercle 


N: axillary nerve


A: external rotation

Deltoid

O: lateral 1/3 of clavicle, acromion, spine of scapula

I: deltoid tuberosity

N: axillary

A: anterior - flexion
 middle - abduction
 posterior - extension

Pectoralis Minor 

O: ribs 3-5

I: coracoid process

N: medial pectoral nerve

A: scapular protraction and depression

Teres Major 

O: inferior angle of scapula
I: medial lip of intertubercle groove
N: lower subscapular nerve
A: adduction and internal rotation  

Pectoralis Major 

O: sternum, upper 6 ribs, and medial 1/2 of clavicle
I: lateral lip of the intertubercle groove
N: medial and lateral pectoral nerves
A: adduction and internal rotation 

Brachioradialis

O: lateral supracondylar ridge


I: radial styloid process


N: radial nerve


A: flexion, pronation, and supination

Corachobrachialis 

O: coracoid procress


I: middle 1/3 of humerus


N: musculocutaneous


A: flexion

Subscapularis 

O: subscapular fossa


I: lesser tubercle


N: upper & lower subscapular
nerves


A: internal rotation

Lower Body 

Illiopsoas

O: Illiacus = inner surface of illium
Psoas = Transverse processes of L1-L5
I: lesser trochanter
N: femoral
A: hip flexion

Sartorius

O: ASIS
I: pes anserinus
N: femoral
A: hip flexion and external rotation

Rectus Femoris

O: AIIS
I: tibial tuberosity
N: femoral
A: extend knee and flex hip

Deep 6 Lateral Rotators

O: sacrum, ischium, and obturator foramen
I: greater trochanter
N: sacral plexus (except obturator externis = obturator nerve)
A: external rotation

Semitendinosus


O: ischial tuberosity

I: pes anserinus

N: tibial

A: extend hip and flex knee

Semimembranosus

O: ischial tuberosity

I: pes anserinus

N: tibial

A: extend hip and flex knee

Bicep Femoris Long Head

O: ischial tuberosity

I: lateral condyle of tibia & head of fibula

N: tibial

A: extend hip and flex knee

Bicep Femoris Short Head 

O: lower 1/2 of linea aspera 

I: lateral condyle of tibia and head of fibula

N: common fibular

A: extend hip and flex knee

Tensor Fascia Latae

O: ASIS

I: Gerdy's tubercle

N: superior gluteal nerve

A: internal rotation and abduction

Gluteus Medius 

O: lateral surface of illium below illiac crest

I: greater trochanter

N: superior gluteal nerve

A: internal rotation and abduction

Gluteus Minimus

O: lateral surface of illium below gluteus medius

I: greater trochanter

N: superior gluteal nerve

A: internal rotation and abduction

Gluteus Maximus 


O: posterior 1/4 of illium, sacrum, and coccyx

I: gluteal line and IT band

N: inferior gluteal nerve

A: hip extension and external rotation

Adductor Brevis

O: inferior pubic ramus

I: pectineal line

N: obturator

A: adduction

Adductor Longus

O: just inferior pubic crest

I: middle 1/3 linea aspera

N: obturator 

A: adduction

Adductor Magnus

O: ramus & ischial tuberosity

I: linea aspera and medial epicondyle of femur

N: obturator & tibial

A: adduction



Gracillis

O: ramus of pubis 

I: pes anserinus 

N: obturator 

A: adduct hip and flex knee

Pectineus

O: pubic crest 

I: pectineal line

N: femoral

A: flexion and adduction

Vastus Lateralis

O: intertrochanteric line, greater trochanter, upper 1/2 of linea aspera

I: tibial tuberosity

N: femoral

A: extend knee

Vastus Medialis

O: linea aspera

I: tibial tuberosity

N: femoral 

A: extend knee

Vastus Intermedius

O: upper 2/3 anterior surface of the femur

I: tibial tuberosity

N: femoral

A: extend knee

Popliteus

O: posterolateral condyle

I: proximomedial posterior tibia

N: tibial

A: internally rotate the tibia (unlock the knee)

Gastrocnemius

O: posterior condyles of femur

I: posterior calcaneus

N: tibial

A: flex knee and plantar flex talocrural joint

Soleus

O: proximal 2/3 tibia and fibula

I: posterior calcaneus

N: tibial

A: plantar flex talocrural

Fibularis Longus

O: head and upper fibula

I: medial cuneiform & 1st metatarsal base

N: superior branch common fibular

A: eversion of subtalar & plantar flex talocrural joint

Fibularis Brevis

O: lower fibula

I: base of 5th metatarsal

N: superior branch of common fibular

A: eversion of subtalar joint & plantar flexion of talocrural joint

Fibularis Tertius

O: distal 1/3 fibula

I: base of 5th metatarsal 

N: deep common fibular nerve

A: eversion of subtalar joint

Tibialis Anterior

O - upper 2/3 tibia

I - medial cuneiform & 1st metatarsal

N - deep branch common fibular

A - dorsiflexion of talocrural & inversion of subtalar

Tibialis Posterior

O - interosseous membrane

I - navicular, 3 cuneiforms, metatarsals 2-5 

N - tibial

A - plantar flex talocrural, inversion

Flexor Hallucis Longus

O - middle 2/3 fibula

I - base of distal phalanx of 1st

N - tibial

A - flex 1st toe

Flexor Digitorum Longus

O - middle 1/3 of the tibia

I - base of distal phalanx 2-5 

N - tibial

A - flex toes 2-5

Extensor Digitorum Longus

O - lateral condyle of tibia, upper 2/3 fibula and head of fibula

I - middle and distal phalanges 2-5

N - deep branch common fibular

A - extend toes 2-5 and dorsiflex talocrural

Extensor Hallucis Longus

O - middle 2/3 fibula

I - distal phalanx of 1st toe

N - deep branch common fibular

A - extend 1st toe and dorsiflex talocrural
